Chicken Quesadilla

A healthy alternative to the famous quesadilla

This version of the chicken quesadilla contains chicken breast and cheddar cheese (or the cheese of your choice), plus a good amount of vegetables.

Use only chicken breast, low fat cheese and little to no souce for a decrease in calories.




Ingredients

  • Flatbread
  • Chicken breast
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Red Pepper
  • Cooked onions
  • Siracha sauce

Steps

  1. Cook chicken breast
  2. Cook flatbread in pan until slightly brown
  3. Shred cheddar on top of flatbread
  4. Place shredded chicken on top of half the flatbread
  5. Place your choice of vegetables
  6. Fold in half
  7. Enjoy!
