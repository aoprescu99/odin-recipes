Egg Bagel

How about something with a little more fat, but still healthy?

This bagel with egg and bacon is the perfect breakfast idea if you want to eat something that's easy to cook, but also delicious.


Ingredients

  • Bagel
  • 2 eggs
  • Bacon
  • Avocado
  • Tomato
  • Slice of cheese

Steps

  1. Cook bagel for 5 minutes in oven until slightly brown
  2. Scramble and cook eggs
  3. Cook bacon
  4. Spread avocado on bagel
  5. Place slice of cheese on top
  6. Place 2 pieces of bacon on top of cheese
  7. Place slice of tomato on top of bacon
  8. Place scrambled eggs on top of bacon
  9. Enjoy!
