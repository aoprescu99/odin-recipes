Protein Pancakes

Healthy recipe for protein pancakes

This recipe is a personal favorite of mine and will help you diversify your repertoire of breakfast recipes.


Ingredients

  • 3 Eggs
  • 1.5 Cups of oats
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 150 grams of greek yogurt
  • 50 - 100 ml of milk
  • 1 banana
  • Agave syrup

Steps

  1. Mix ingredients in blender or by hand (leave out the fruit and syrup)
  2. Spray heated pan (low - medium heat) with cooking oil
  3. Pour first batch (you should be able to easily flip the pancake with a spatula)
  4. Stack pancakes
  5. Cut banana and place the pieces on top of the stack of pancakes
  6. Cover with agave syrup
  7. Enjoy!
